Who am I?

TL;DR: I love coding, in particular with JavaScript, and I love building fast, performant, rock solid web interfaces/applications for the desktop and mobile. I like challenges and I'm eager to learn new things and to improve every day.

I'm a passionate front-end/UI/UX engineer, computer science grad from Padua(Italy) and I've been doing (and loving) professionally this since the university years (~2004/2007). This means I can design, code, test and deliver complex web interfaces/applications within an extremely small amount of time. Being this kind of software engineer thrills me and I love building awesome interfaces for the web from scratch. I also have a deep passion for clean design, performance and speed and I think my personal mission is to make complex interfaces easy to use also for my 90 y.o. grandma.

I eat JavaScript and Ruby for breakfast and I'm a huge Ruby on Rails, MooTools, jQuery and Bootstrap fan. Lately I'm enjoying a lot facebook's react, node.js and mobile/responsive performance and design. Moreover, during my job and my spare time, I research/test CSS3 and HTML5 stuff on a tons of web/mobile browsers and I also code with SASS, HAML, CoffeeScript and sometimes - remembering my university years - C, C++ and Python.
I can also develop full-stack applications (using sql/nosql persistence) and I also used to be a PHP and Java programmer ... and I'm not ashamed of it :P ... mmm, well, I think I should be a little bit ashamed, but whatever :D
I use(d, because of lately I have a very small amount of free time) to be an open source contributor and a stackoverflow active member.
